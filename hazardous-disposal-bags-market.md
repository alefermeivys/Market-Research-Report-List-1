<p><p>The hazardous disposal bags market is primarily categorized into three types based on material: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene). </p><p>HDPE bags are known for their strength and rigidity, making them suitable for heavy waste. LDPE bags offer flexibility and are often used for less hazardous materials. LLDPE bags combine the benefits of both HDPE and LDPE, providing enhanced puncture resistance and elasticity, making them ideal for a variety of hazardous waste applications.</p></p>
