<p><p>The dental tools and equipment market is categorized into several key types. Examination instruments include mirrors and explorers for diagnosis. Prosthodontic instruments help in the creation of dentures and crowns. Extraction instruments facilitate tooth removal, while endodontic instruments, including root canal files, aid in treating dental pulp issues. Each type plays a crucial role in dental care.</p></p>
