<p><p>Espresso coffee makers are categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require baristas to control every aspect of the brewing process, offering a hands-on experience. Semi-automatic machines automate water delivery while allowing users to manage grind size and tamping. Fully automatic models take convenience further by automating grinding, brewing, and sometimes even milk frothing, making them ideal for users seeking speed and consistency with minimal effort. Each type caters to different brewing preferences and expertise levels.</p></p>
