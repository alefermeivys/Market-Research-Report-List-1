<p><ul><li>Linear Disposable Surgical Stapler</li><li>Circular Disposable Surgical Stapler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable surgical stapler market is categorized into two main types: linear disposable surgical staplers and circular disposable surgical staplers. Linear staplers are designed for creating linear rows of staples, ideal for procedures like bowel resections and wound closures. In contrast, circular staplers are used to connect and create anastomoses in circular patterns, commonly employed in surgeries like gastrointestinal procedures. Both types enhance surgical efficiency, reduce operation time, and minimize patient trauma, making them essential in modern surgical practices.</p></p>
