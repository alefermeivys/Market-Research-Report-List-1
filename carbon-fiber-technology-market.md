<p><ul><li>PAN</li><li>Pitch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber technology market primarily consists of two types: PAN (Polyacrylonitrile) and Pitch-based carbon fibers. PAN fibers dominate the market due to their excellent mechanical properties and versatility, making them suitable for various applications in aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods. Pitch-based carbon fibers, derived from petroleum or coal tar pitch, offer higher thermal conductivity and are often used in specialized applications, such as in electronics and high-performance components. Each type serves distinct industry needs based on performance characteristics.</p></p>
