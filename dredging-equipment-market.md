<p><p>The dredging equipment market comprises various types that serve different purposes in material removal from water bodies. Mechanical dredgers utilize buckets or clamshells to scoop sediments, ideal for handling large debris. Hydraulic dredgers, on the other hand, use high-pressure water jets to create a slurry, efficiently removing fine materials. Other dredgers may include specialized equipment like vacuum dredgers or cutter-suction dredgers, each tailored for specific applications such as environmental restoration, construction, or maintenance of waterways.</p></p>
