<p><p>The hydraulic rubber hose market is categorized into high, medium, and low pressure hoses. High pressure rubber hoses are designed to withstand extreme pressures, typically exceeding 2,500 psi, making them ideal for heavy-duty industrial applications. Medium pressure hoses operate in the range of 1,000 to 2,500 psi, suitable for various hydraulic systems. Low pressure hoses, generally below 1,000 psi, are used in lighter applications, such as fluid transfer in agriculture and automotive fields. Each type serves distinct operational needs and applications.</p></p>
