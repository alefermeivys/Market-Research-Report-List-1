<p><p>The water-free and waterless urinals market includes four main types: </p><p>1. **Liquid Sealant Cartridges**: These systems use a liquid sealant to create a barrier that prevents odors while allowing urine to pass through.</p><p>2. **Membrane Traps**: These employ a membrane that effectively separates urine from air, minimizing odors and preventing backflow.</p><p>3. **Biological Blocks**: These involve biodegradable materials that break down waste and neutralize odors naturally.</p><p>4. **Mechanical Balls**: These utilize floating balls that seal the urinal's drain, blocking odors while allowing urine to flow. These systems promote water conservation and sustainability.</p></p>
