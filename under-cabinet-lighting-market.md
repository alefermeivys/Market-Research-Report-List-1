<p><strong>The Under Cabinet Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incandescent</li><li>Fluorescent</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Under cabinet lighting encompasses three main types: incandescent, fluorescent, and LED. Incandescent lights offer warm tones with a traditional feel but are less energy-efficient and have shorter lifespans. Fluorescent lights provide brighter, cooler illumination and are more energy-efficient than incandescent but may require warm-up time and have less appealing color rendering. LED lights are the most energy-efficient option, boasting long lifespans, low heat emission, and a variety of color options, making them a popular choice for modern kitchens and workspaces.</p></p>
