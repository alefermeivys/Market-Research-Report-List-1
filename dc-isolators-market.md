<p><strong>The DC Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Break Isolator</li><li>Double Break Isolator</li><li>Pantograph type Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC Isolators market includes three main types: single break isolators, double break isolators, and pantograph type isolators. Single break isolators utilize one contact point for disconnecting, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Double break isolators feature two contact points, providing enhanced safety and reliability by ensuring complete isolation. Pantograph type isolators are designed for overhead applications, enabling quick connection and disconnection of transmission lines. Each type serves specific requirements in power transmission and distribution, catering to diverse market demands.</p></p>
