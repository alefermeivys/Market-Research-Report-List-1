<p><strong>The Medical Occluder Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ASD Occluder</li><li>VSD Occluder</li><li>PDA Occluder</li><li>PFO Occluder</li><li>LAA Occluder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical occluder device market features various types designed to treat congenital and structural heart defects. ASD occluders address atrial septal defects, while VSD occluders are used for ventricular septal defects. PDA occluders close patent ductus arteriosus, and PFO occluders resolve patent foramen ovale. LAA occluders target left atrial appendage isolation, primarily to reduce stroke risk in atrial fibrillation patients. Each device serves a specific purpose, contributing to improved patient outcomes in cardiac care.</p></p>
