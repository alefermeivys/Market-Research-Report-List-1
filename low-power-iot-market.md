<p><p>Low Power IoT market comprises three main types: NB-IoT, LoRa, and LTE-M. NB-IoT is designed for deep indoor coverage and handles a vast number of connected devices, ideal for smart cities and agriculture. LoRa utilizes unlicensed spectrum for long-range communication with low power consumption, suitable for environmental monitoring and asset tracking. LTE-M provides higher bandwidth and mobility support, making it suitable for applications like wearables and fleet management. Together, they optimize connectivity for diverse IoT applications.</p></p>
