<p><strong>The Extreme Ultraviolet Lithography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Produced Plasmas(LPP)</li><li>Vacuum Sparks</li><li>Gas Discharges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extreme ultraviolet lithography (EUVL) market encompasses various plasma generation methods for semiconductor manufacturing. Laser Produced Plasmas (LPP) utilize high-energy lasers to create plasma, producing the intense EUV light needed for photolithography. Vacuum Sparks generate plasma by discharging high-voltage arcs in a vacuum, offering a controlled environment for EUV production. Gas Discharges involve ionizing gases to produce plasma, delivering another avenue for EUV light generation. Each method contributes to the advancement of EUVL technology, essential for fabricating smaller, more powerful chips.</p></p>
