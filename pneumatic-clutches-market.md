<p><strong>The Pneumatic Clutches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Friction Type</li><li>Toothed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic clutches market comprises primarily two types: friction type and toothed type. Friction type clutches use surface friction to engage and disengage, providing smooth operation and high torque transmission, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Toothed type clutches, on the other hand, utilize interlocking teeth to create a positive engagement, offering precise control and quick response times. Both types cater to different operational needs, with friction clutches focusing on smoothness and toothed clutches emphasizing accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
