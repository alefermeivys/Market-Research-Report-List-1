<p><p>Solar pumps for community water supply primarily come in two types: submersible and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to be submerged in water sources like wells, effectively lifting water to the surface. They're efficient for deep water extraction. Surface pumps, on the other hand, are installed above ground and draw water from nearby sources, such as lakes or rivers. Both types utilize solar energy, making them sustainable solutions for enhancing water accessibility in rural and underserved areas.</p></p>
