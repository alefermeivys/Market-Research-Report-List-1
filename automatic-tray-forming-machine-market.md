<p><p>The Automatic Tray Forming Machine Market is primarily segmented into two types: Vertical Form-Fill-Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form-Fill-Seal (H-FFS). V-FFS machines operate in a vertical orientation, forming and sealing pouches from a single roll of film, making them ideal for high-speed packaging of lightweight products. In contrast, H-FFS machines work horizontally, suitable for packaging bulkier items and ensuring precise filling and sealing. Each type caters to different industry needs, enhancing efficiency and product protection.</p></p>
