<p><p>The C9 hydrogenated resin market comprises two primary segments: monomer and copolymer. Monomer resins are derived from a single type of aromatic hydrocarbon, offering strong adhesion and thermal stability, making them suitable for applications like inks and coatings. In contrast, copolymer resins result from combining different monomer types, enhancing flexibility and performance characteristics. These copolymers are often utilized in adhesives and sealants, providing improved elasticity and durability. Together, these market types cater to diverse industrial needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
