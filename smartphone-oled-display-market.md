<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED) Dispaly</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED) Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smartphone OLED display market comprises two main types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) displays. AMOLED displays use a matrix of individual pixels controlled by active transistors, providing superior picture quality, faster refresh rates, and lower power consumption, making them popular in high-end smartphones. In contrast, PMOLED technology employs a simpler approach with rows and columns of pixels, ideal for smaller screens and less complex applications, but with limitations in size and display quality.</p></p>
