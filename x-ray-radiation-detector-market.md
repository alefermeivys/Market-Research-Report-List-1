<p><p>The X-Ray Radiation Detector market encompasses various technologies, each catering to specific imaging needs. Flat Panel Detectors are widely used in digital radiography for their high image quality and efficiency. Computed Radiography (CR) Detectors utilize a photostimulable phosphor to capture images, offering a transition from film-based systems. Charge Coupled Device (CCD) Detectors provide excellent sensitivity and resolution, commonly found in dental and small animal imaging. Other detectors may include portable systems or specialized applications, enhancing versatility in medical and industrial fields.</p></p>
