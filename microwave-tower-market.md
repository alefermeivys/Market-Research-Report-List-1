<p><p>The microwave tower market encompasses various types of structures essential for communication systems. Angle steel towers are robust, designed for heavy loads and greater heights, ideal for rural and remote installations. Steel tube towers offer a lighter, more aerodynamic alternative, suitable for urban environments. Single-pipe towers are cost-effective and easy to install, often used for low-height applications. Mast towers, known for their slender profiles, are used in locations where space is limited but require significant elevation for line-of-sight transmission.</p></p>
