<p><p>Epoxy resin glass fiber prepreg is categorized into three curing types based on temperature: low, medium, and high. Low-temperature curing prepregs are designed for applications requiring less thermal stress and typically cure at around 120°C, suitable for electronic components. Medium-temperature curing prepregs operate between 140°C to 180°C, providing a balance of performance and processing ease for automotive and aerospace components. High-temperature curing prepregs exceed 180°C, ideal for demanding applications like aerospace and industrial sectors, offering superior thermal resistance and mechanical strength.</p></p>
