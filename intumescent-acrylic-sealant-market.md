<p><p>The Intumescent Acrylic Sealant market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based sealants are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and have low VOC emissions, making them ideal for indoor use. They provide effective fire protection while offering flexibility and paintability. In contrast, solvent-based sealants offer superior adherence and durability, making them suitable for harsh outdoor environments. They typically deliver better fire resistance but may have higher VOC levels, requiring careful handling and application. Both types serve crucial roles in fire safety and building compliance.</p></p>
