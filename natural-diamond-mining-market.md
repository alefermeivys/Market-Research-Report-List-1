<p><ul><li>Underground Mining</li><li>Open Pit Mining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural diamond mining market primarily comprises two types: underground mining and open pit mining. Underground mining involves extracting diamonds from deep within the Earth's crust, utilizing shaft systems and tunnels to access deposits, leading to less surface disturbance but higher operational costs. In contrast, open pit mining involves removing layers of soil and rock to access diamond deposits closer to the surface, making it more economically viable for larger deposits, but it can cause significant environmental impact.</p></p>
