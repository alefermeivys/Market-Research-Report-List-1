<p><ul><li>Inert Glass Powders</li><li>Reactive Glass Powders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental glass powders market is categorized into two main types: inert glass powders and reactive glass powders. Inert glass powders are chemically stable and primarily used as fillers in dental materials, providing strength and aesthetic qualities without participating in chemical reactions. Reactive glass powders, on the other hand, engage in chemical interactions with other components during curing processes, enhancing bonding and bioactivity. Both types play crucial roles in dental restorations and prosthetics, contributing to overall material performance and patient outcomes.</p></p>
