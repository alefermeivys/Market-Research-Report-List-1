<p><p>The metal forming tool market encompasses various equipment essential for shaping metal. Bending machines are utilized to create bends and angles in metal sheets, allowing for precise dimensional control. Shearing machines cut through metal sheets or plates with shear force, ensuring clean edges for further processing. Forging machines apply compressive forces to mold metal into desired shapes, enhancing strength and durability. Together, these machines play a crucial role in manufacturing, construction, and automotive industries, driving efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
