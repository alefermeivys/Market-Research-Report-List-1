<p><p>The safety needles and syringes market comprises two main types: active and passive safety needles. Active safety needles require the user to engage a mechanism to activate safety features, enhancing protection after use. In contrast, passive safety needles automatically shield the needle after injection without user intervention, minimizing the risk of needle-stick injuries. Both types aim to improve safety for healthcare professionals and patients by reducing exposure to hazardous materials and promoting better waste management in medical settings.</p></p>
