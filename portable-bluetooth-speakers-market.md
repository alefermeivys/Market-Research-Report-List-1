<p><ul><li>AC/DC Bluetooth speakers</li><li>AC-only Bluetooth speakers</li><li>DC-only Bluetooth speakers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable Bluetooth speakers market can be categorized into three main types: AC/DC Bluetooth speakers, AC-only Bluetooth speakers, and DC-only Bluetooth speakers. AC/DC Bluetooth speakers are versatile, functioning with both alternating and direct current, allowing for use at home or on the go. AC-only Bluetooth speakers rely solely on electrical outlets, making them ideal for stationary use. In contrast, DC-only Bluetooth speakers run on battery power, offering true portability for outdoor activities or travel, appealing to a wide range of consumers.</p></p>
