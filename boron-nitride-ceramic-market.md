<p><strong>The Boron Nitride Ceramic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Boron Nitride Ceramic market is typically categorized into two primary types: Type I and Type II. Type I comprises hexagonal boron nitride, known for its lubricating properties, high thermal conductivity, and electrical insulation characteristics. It is commonly used in electronics and aerospace applications. Type II, on the other hand, includes cubic boron nitride, which is harder and more thermally stable than diamond, making it ideal for cutting tools and abrasive materials. Each type serves distinct industrial purposes based on its unique properties.</p></p>
